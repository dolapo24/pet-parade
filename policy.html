Pet Parade is committed to providing a safe and welcoming environment for all pets and their owners. To ensure the health and happiness of our furry friends, we have established the following policies:

  1. All pets must be up-to-date on their vaccinations and have a current rabies tag. Owners must provide proof of vaccination upon request.
  2. All pets must be leashed or in a carrier while in the store, unless they are participating in a supervised event.
  3. Owners are responsible for cleaning up after their pets. We provide waste bags and disposal stations for your convenience.
  4. Aggressive or disruptive pets may be asked to leave the store at the discretion of our staff.
  5. Children under the age of 12 must be supervised by an adult at all times.
  6. We reserve the right to refuse service to anyone who violates our policies or behaves in a disruptive or disrespectful manner.
By visiting Pet Parade, you agree to abide by these policies and help us create a safe and enjoyable experience for all pets and their owners.
